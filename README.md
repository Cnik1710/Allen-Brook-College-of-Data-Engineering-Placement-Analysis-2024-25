## üìà Methodology & Analysis  
### **Prepare, Process & Analytical Approach:** 
  ‚Ä¢ **Removed missing or inconsistent values** to ensure clean and reliable data. 
  ‚Ä¢ **Standardized categorical fields** such as **Degree Type** and **Specialization** for uniformity.

### **Top Findings:** 
* ‚Ä¢ **Placement Rate**: 69% of students (148 out of 215) were successfully placed.
* ‚Ä¢ **Gender Gap**: Males earned a higher average salary (‚Çπ2.15 LPA) compared to females (‚Çπ1.69 LPA).
* ‚Ä¢ **Academic Background**: Students from **Science & Tech** backgrounds achieved the **highest average salary (~‚Çπ2.19 LPA)**.

* ‚Ä¢ **CGPA Correlation**: Salary increased steadily with CGPA ‚Äî students with **8‚Äì9 CGPA** earned an **average ‚Çπ3.45 LPA**.
* ‚Ä¢ **Work Experience**: Students with prior work experience showed **slightly higher placement success and pay levels**.

### **Supporting Metrics:**

### **Summary:**

  * The interactive dashboard delivers a **concise, data-driven overview** of college placement performance ‚Äî enabling administrators to refine **career support strategies**, align **curriculum with industry trends**, and help students understand **key drivers of salary and employability**.
